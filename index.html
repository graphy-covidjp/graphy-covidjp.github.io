Forward

Hi! This is a collection of COVID-19 related graphs that I've cobbled together and found interesting. Although it's very unclear how useful any of these statistics are, I can't stop watching them, so I've made this webpage automatically update with the latest data I can find.

The data is gathered from a variety of sources, and as such any one of them may flake out and cause unpredictable things to happen on this page.

The "Minute Physics"-style graphs on this page are inspired by this excellent video. I've added some extra coloring cues and skipped the animation, but the core idea is the same.
